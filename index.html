Universidad de Chile's RoboCup teams

The SPL team has been the fourth best team in the league by three years in a row. It is formed by about 15 people pursuing bachelor, masters and doctorate programs in electrical engineering and computer science.

Research developed by the team is related to perception, localization, decision making and multi-robot systems with resources-constrained platforms in highly dynamic environments.

SPL Team

UChile Homebreakers is the RoboCup@Home team of the university. Its service robot, Bender, was built from scratch in 2007, being in permanent development until today.

The main challenges faced by the team involve from robot building, design and electronic development to high-level perception, navigation and planning, towards human-robot interaction (HRI)
